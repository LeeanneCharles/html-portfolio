The Best Movies According to Leeanne

My Top 3 Favorite Movies of All Time

Pride and Prejudice

PG 2005
Romance/Comedy

Regency love story showcasing chemisrty between a handsome upper-class man and an outspoken countryside eldest sister.

Tangled

PG 2010
Family/Musical

A thief stumbles upon a kidnapped princess that has been locked away in a tower. He makes a deal with her to bring her to see the floating lanterns. As their connection/chemistry grows, they descover the truth about the stolen princess.

The Mummy

PG-13 1999
Adventure/Action

A rousing, suspenseful and horrifying epic about an expedition of treasure seekers goes wrong when stumbling upon an ancient Egyptian Tomb.
